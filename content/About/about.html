Who we are

New York AREA is an alliance of New York businesses, labor unions, environmental and social leaders, and independent energy experts. We are united in our commitment to support policies to ensure that our state has the reliable and affordable electricity it needs to grow our economy and maintain New York's status as a global center for life and business.

We share the challenges of providing energy in New York and advocate for smart solutions that grow the economy and create jobs by meeting our state's energy needs.

New York AREA, a non-profit trade organization since 2003, is dedicated to ensuring a bright future for New York with affordable and reliable electricity.
